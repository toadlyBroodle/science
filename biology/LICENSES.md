## Safe-to-commit pipeline contract

When `scripts/download.py` and `scripts/convert.py` run,
they populate `sources/{html,pdf,md}/`, which are
gitignored by default. If you want to commit converted
full-text markdown, restrict the commit to sources marked
**redistributable** above, and include the license + DOI
as YAML front matter in the committed `.md` file.
