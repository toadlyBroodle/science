# Urolithin A immune rejuvenation RCT

## Summary
Randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled trial: **urolithin A
(UA) 1000 mg/day** in **50 healthy middle-aged adults** for **4 weeks**.

## Mechanism
UA is a gut-microbial metabolite (of ellagic acid → ellagitannins) that
**induces mitophagy** — selective autophagy of damaged mitochondria.
Mitochondrial dysfunction is a [[topics/hallmarks-of-aging]].

## Key findings
- UA **expands peripheral naive-like, less terminally exhausted CD8+
  T cells**.
- Increases **CD8+ fatty-acid oxidation capacity**.
- Translates mouse-level mitophagy/longevity benefits into human
  immunological endpoints.

## Why this matters
Clean RCT evidence for a dietary metabolite moving an immune-aging
biomarker. Pairs well with any [[topics/aging-clocks]]-based reanalysis.
