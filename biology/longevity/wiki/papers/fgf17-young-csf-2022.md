# FGF17 in young CSF rejuvenates aged brain

## Summary
Demonstrates that **infusing young cerebrospinal fluid** into aged
mouse brains **restores memory**. Drills down to **FGF17**, sourced
by youthful neurons, as the key mediator.

## Mechanism
- Transcriptomic analysis identifies **oligodendrocytes** as the most
  CSF-responsive cell type.
- Young CSF / FGF17 boosts **oligodendrocyte progenitor cell (OPC)**
  proliferation and differentiation in the aged hippocampus.
- **SRF** (serum response factor) mediates OPC proliferation
  downstream of FGF17.

## Why it's a landmark
First clean demonstration that a defined CSF factor rejuvenates brain
function. Establishes CSF as a therapeutic compartment distinct from
blood.
