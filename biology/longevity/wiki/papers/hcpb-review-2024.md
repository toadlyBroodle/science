# Heterochronic parabiosis — 2024 review

## Summary
Review of the **heterochronic parabiosis (HCPB)** literature: surgical
anastomosis of young and old circulatory systems, used for ~150 years
to identify systemic aging factors.

## Factors covered
- **GDF11** — abundant young, declines with age; injection restores
  muscle regeneration, mitochondrial morphology, NMJ size.
- **CCL11** — elevated in aged plasma/CSF; promotes senescence via
  pro-oxidant/pro-inflammatory SASP.
- **Klotho, GDF15, Oxytocin, TIMP2** — shorter profiles.

## 2024 additions
- **PEDF** (see [[papers/pedf-parabiosis-2024]]).
- Revisit of Tabula Muris Consortium scRNA-seq HCPB data showing
  young blood reverses age-related global loss of expression.

## Why a CS person should care
Each factor is a single molecular entry point into rejuvenation.
Combined with [[papers/clockbase-agent-2025]], the full dose-response
landscape of these factors across public data is an open project.
