# Young ADSC exosomes rejuvenate old mice

## Summary
Injection of **small extracellular vesicles (sEVs) from young
adipose-derived stem cells (ADSCs)** into old mice:

- Improved motor coordination, grip strength, fatigue resistance.
- Restored fur regeneration, improved renal function.
- Drove pro-regenerative effects and reduced oxidative stress,
  inflammation, and senescence markers.
- **Decreased epigenetic age** ([[topics/aging-clocks]] readout).

## Why this is a landmark
Non-cellular, cell-free therapy that ports the young-stem-cell benefit
into a shelf-stable modality. Exosomes are the current frontier for
stem-cell-adjacent rejuvenation ([[topics/exosomes-extracellular-vesicles]]).
