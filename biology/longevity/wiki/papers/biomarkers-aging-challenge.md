# Biomarkers of Aging Consortium — Challenge portal

Live portal for the multi-phase open competition described in
[[papers/nunez2024-open-competition]].

## Phase structure
1. Chronological-age prediction (Mar–Jul 2024, $30k) — closed.
2. Mortality / longevity prediction (Jul–Nov 2024, $70k) — closed.
3. **Healthspan / multi-morbidity prediction — Q3 2025 onward, $100k.**

## Platform
Competition is hosted on **Synapse** (Sage Bionetworks), not Kaggle.
Consortium site links the current active phase.
