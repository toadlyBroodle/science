# SINGULAR — Cell Rejuvenation Atlas

## Summary
**SINGULAR** = Single-cell RNA-seq Investigation of Rejuvenation
Agents and Longevity. Unified network-biology analysis of **6
rejuvenation strategies across 9 studies, 73 cell types**, at
single-cell resolution.

## Method
For each strategy, SINGULAR characterises effects at multiple levels:
- gene regulatory networks
- intracellular signalling
- cell-cell communication
- cellular processes

Then identifies **master regulators** that orchestrate the rejuvenation
response and compares which are conserved across strategies.

## Why a CS person should care
Rejuvenation is studied in a balkanised way — every lab's strategy
gets its own atlas. SINGULAR is the first systems-biology framework
that **compares them on a common footing**, which means you can ask
questions like "which master regulator is invariant across chemical
cocktails + OSK + young blood + exercise?" and get an answer.

## Output
Drug-mimicry selection: given a complex intervention's master-regulator
signature, SINGULAR ranks pharmaceuticals likely to mimic it. This is
a concrete prediction task you can extend.
