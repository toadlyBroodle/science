# An Open Competition for Biomarkers of Aging

## Summary
Multi-phase open-science competition launched March 2024 by the
[[topics/biomarkers-of-aging]] Consortium. 152 teams from 28 countries
competed on chronological-age prediction and mortality prediction.

## Dataset
- Novel DNA methylation profiles generated on **Illumina MethylationEPIC
  v2.0** from **500 blood samples** (Mass General Brigham Biobank), ages
  18–99, balanced for sex.
- Paired proteomic profiles and longitudinal health outcomes for the
  same cohort.
- Hosted on Synapse (not Kaggle).

## Phases
1. **Chronological Age Prediction** — Mar 1 → Jul 5 2024, $30k prize
2. **Mortality Prediction** — Jul 1 → Nov 1 2024, $70k prize
3. **Multi-morbidity / Healthspan Prediction** — Q3 2025 onward, $100k

## Why a CS person should care
Closest analogue to Kaggle for aging. Fixed dataset, objective scoring,
public leaderboard, real prize money, explicit benchmark — and the
Phase 3 target (which diseases hit which people) is exactly the
supervised-learning problem ML people already know how to attack.
