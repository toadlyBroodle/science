# TERT knock-in extends lifespan

## Summary
**TertKI** mice with EF1α-Tert knocked into the Rosa26 locus show
**elevated telomerase activity, elongated telomeres, and extended
lifespan** — without the tumorigenicity historically associated with
telomerase overexpression.

## Context
Complements the 2012 Bernardes de Jesus AAV9-TERT study (+24% median
lifespan at 1y, +13% at 2y). This 2025 paper uses a cleaner genetic
handle and adds DNA-damage-repair readouts.

## Note
A separate 2022 PNAS paper (MCMV-TERT) reported +41.4% median lifespan
but **was retracted in August 2025**. Be careful citing it.

## Why a CS person should care
Telomerase is the single most "classical" anti-aging target. Good
modern knock-in baseline for any ML-driven gene-therapy prioritization.
