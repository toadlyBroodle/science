# D+Q + Fisetin vs. methylation clocks

## Summary
Phase I longitudinal study: **19 participants** took dasatinib +
quercetin (DQ) for **6 months**, with DNA methylation measured at
baseline, 3 months, and 6 months. A follow-up arm added fisetin (DQF).

## Why read
Direct empirical test of whether a senolytic regimen moves human
epigenetic-age estimates. Bridges [[topics/senolytics]] and
[[topics/aging-clocks]].
