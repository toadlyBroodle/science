# STAMINA — senolytics for MCI + slow gait (2025)

## Summary
**STAMINA** = Senolytics To Alleviate Mobility Issues and Neurological
Impairments in Ageing. 12-week single-arm pilot in **12 adults ≥65y**
with slow gait speed + mild cognitive impairment (MCI) — two
precursors of Alzheimer's.

## Protocol
- **100 mg dasatinib + 1250 mg quercetin** once daily on **2
  consecutive days every 2 weeks**, 6 cycles over 12 weeks.

## Significance
First human readout of D+Q against actual cognition/mobility endpoints
in a pre-AD population.
