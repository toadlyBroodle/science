# SB000 — a single factor for safer cellular rejuvenation

## Summary
Shift Bioscience identified **SB000**, the first single-gene
intervention shown to rejuvenate cells from multiple germ layers with
efficacy rivalling Yamanaka OSK(M), **without activating pluripotency
pathways**.

## Why this matters for CS
The whole result was generated by **training a transcriptomic ageing
clock and then screening for genes that, when overexpressed, minimise
it** — without selecting for pluripotency. Screen granularity and scale
exceed any prior reprogramming work. This is a direct template for
CS-led intervention discovery.

## Method (as we understand it)
1. Train a transcriptomic aging clock on public bulk RNA-seq.
2. For each candidate gene, predict post-perturbation transcriptome
   from a generative model and score via the clock.
3. Top hits are experimentally tested in fibroblasts (and keratinocytes)
   for clock reversal + pluripotency pathway activation.

## Key findings
- **SB000 reverses epigenetic + transcriptomic age** in human
  fibroblasts while preserving fibroblast identity.
- No c-MYC-like pluripotency signature.
- Rejuvenation **generalises to keratinocytes** with potency
  matching/surpassing OSK(M).
- Two weeks of expression is sufficient for transcriptomic age reversal.
