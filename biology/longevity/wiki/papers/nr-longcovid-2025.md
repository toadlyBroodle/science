# NR for long-COVID — RCT

## Summary
Double-blind placebo-controlled RCT: **nicotinamide riboside (NR)
2000 mg/day** vs. placebo in **58 long-COVID patients** for **24
weeks**. Primary endpoints: NAD+ levels, cognition, symptom recovery.

## Why include here
- Clinical-scale test of NAD+ precursor supplementation.
- Long-COVID is increasingly modelled as an accelerated-aging /
  mitochondrial-dysfunction condition, making this indirectly relevant
  to [[topics/nad-mitophagy]] in healthy aging.
