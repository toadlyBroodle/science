# Chemical cocktails reverse cellular aging

## Summary
First small-molecule reprogramming approach that **reverses the
transcriptomic age of human cells** without requiring gene therapy.
From David Sinclair's lab (Harvard) in collaboration with Gladyshev,
published in *Aging (Albany NY)*, July 2023.

## Screen design
Of **80** candidate cocktails tested, **6** statistically improved
nucleocytoplasmic compartmentalisation (NCC) within four days.

## Top hits
- The most potent cocktail: **VC6TF**.
- **Sodium butyrate** (HDAC inhibitor) is one of the most effective
  additives in both human and mouse contexts.
- Each cocktail is 5–7 small molecules.

## Why a CS person should care
The underlying assay is a transcriptome-level age reversal readout, so
larger CS-driven chemical screens are a natural follow-on. See
[[papers/lipid-droplets-reprog-2025]] for a safety caveat on the
in-vivo version.
