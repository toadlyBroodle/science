# XPRIZE Healthspan

## Summary
**7-year, $101M** competition (launched Nov 2023) challenging teams to
restore muscle, cognition, and immune function by **≥10 years
(stretch goal 20)** in **1 year** in **50–80 year-olds**.

## Prize structure
- **+20 years** of function → **$81M**
- **+15 years** → **$71M**
- **+10 years** → **$61M**

## Status (2025)
- 600+ teams from 58 countries registered.
- Top 100 semifinalists announced.
- Top 40 Healthspan + top 8 FSHD Bonus teams received **$250k each**
  as Milestone 1 awards to fund early-stage clinical trials.

## Why it matters for CS
The evaluation requires teams to demonstrate improvement via
**biomarker tests** ([[topics/biomarkers-of-aging]] such as blood-based
clocks). This creates a clean, high-stakes dataset of matched
pre-/post-intervention biomarker panels across hundreds of interventions
— the kind of ground truth that makes ML-driven meta-analysis viable.
