# Metabolomic profile of biological aging in UK Biobank (n = 250,341)

## Summary
Systematic survey of **325 NMR biomarkers** (Nightingale Health platform)
in **250,341** UK Biobank participants — identifies a **54-biomarker**
aging-associated signature tied to all-cause mortality.

## Key findings
- **54 representative NMR biomarkers** associated with mortality
- GWAS on 325 biomarkers using WGS for **95,372** participants
- **Multivariable Mendelian randomization** → **439** candidate
  biomarker-disease causal pairs (nominal significance)
- Released metabolomic aging score **outperforms** other aging metrics
  for short-term mortality prediction
- Longitudinal subset (**n = 13,263**) enables a **metabolomic aging
  rate** — identifies candidate anti-aging and pro-aging biomarkers

## Why a CS person should care
Nightingale NMR is released as part of UKB bulk data. Causal-inference
tooling (MR, G-estimation) is well-suited to this panel. Rate-of-aging
time-series modeling on the longitudinal subset is under-explored.
