# Partial cellular reprogramming — 2024 review

## Summary
Comprehensive review of partial reprogramming as a rejuvenation
platform. Covers OSKM / OSK / cyclic induction / chemical variants,
in-vivo tissue targeting (retina, skeletal muscle, heart, liver,
brain, intestine), and the pluripotency-vs-rejuvenation tradeoff.

## Why read first
Best single entry point to the partial-reprogramming field as of 2024.

## Key ideas surveyed
- **Cyclic induction** (e.g. 2 days ON, 5 days OFF) supports long-term
  reprogramming up to 35 cycles / 10 months safely.
- Tissue-specific rejuvenation papers are piling up but lack common
  endpoints — see [[papers/singular-rejuv-atlas-2024]] for the CS
  response.
- Safety/pluripotency is the dominant bottleneck — see
  [[papers/shift-sb000-2025]] for the current frontier.
