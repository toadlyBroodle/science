# AAV-OSK extends lifespan in old mice

## Summary
Demonstrates that systemic AAV delivery of an **inducible OSK** (Oct4,
Sox2, Klf4 — no c-Myc) into **124-week-old male mice** extends **median
remaining lifespan by 109%** vs. wild-type controls, and improves
multiple health parameters.

## Why it's landmark
First organ-agnostic gene-therapy partial reprogramming study with a
clean lifespan endpoint in late-life mice. Proof of concept that in-vivo
OSK is compatible with safety over a therapeutic window.

## Caveats
Male-only cohort, specific OSK dosing cycle, and requires inducible
tet-on machinery. Translation to humans requires solving vector /
doxycycline dependency.
