# Telomeres and telomerase

Telomeres cap chromosomes; they shorten with each division, and their
attrition is one of the 12 [[topics/hallmarks-of-aging]]. Telomerase
(TERT) maintains them.

## In this wiki
- [[papers/tert-knockin-2025]] — genetic TERT overexpression extends
  lifespan.

## Caution
- A 2022 PNAS paper (MCMV-TERT, +41.4% lifespan) was **retracted in
  August 2025**. Do not cite.
