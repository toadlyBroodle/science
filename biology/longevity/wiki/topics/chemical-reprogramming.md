# Chemical reprogramming

Small-molecule cocktails (HDAC inhibitors, pathway modulators, etc.)
that partially rejuvenate cells without requiring gene therapy.

## Flagship
- [[papers/yang-chemical-cocktails-2023]] — Sinclair lab, 6 cocktails,
  VC6TF most potent.
- [[papers/chemical-reprog-lifespan-2025]] — lifespan-level demonstration.

## Safety caveat
- [[papers/lipid-droplets-reprog-2025]] — in-vivo lipid-droplet
  accumulation hinders rejuvenation.
