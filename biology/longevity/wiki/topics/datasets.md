# Datasets (what you can actually download)

## Mouse lifespan
- [[papers/itp-mpd-portal]] — ITP per-cohort Kaplan–Meier data (open).
- [[papers/dr-960-mice-nature-2024]] — 960 DO mice, deep phenotyping
  (supplementary / GEO).

## DNA methylation
- [[papers/computagebench]] — 66 harmonized public datasets on
  HuggingFace.
- [[papers/nunez2024-open-competition]] — 500-sample MethylationEPIC
  v2.0 dataset (Synapse).

## UK Biobank (gated)
- [[topics/uk-biobank]] — NMR metabolomics, Olink proteomics, WGS.

## Transcriptomic reanalysis sources
- GTEx, TCGA, Tabula Muris Senis, LINCS L1000, GEO partial-reprogramming
  series.
- [[papers/clockbase-agent-2025]] — aggregates ~2M public methylation
  + RNA-seq samples with ~40 aging-clock predictions.
- [[papers/singular-rejuv-atlas-2024]] — unified scRNA-seq view of 6
  rejuvenation strategies (73 cell types).

## Drug / chemistry
- DrugBank, ChEMBL, LOPAC-1280, Prestwick, TCMbank
  — used in [[papers/smerbarreto2023-senolytics]],
  [[papers/senolytic-predictor-2025]], [[papers/network-repurposing-aging]].
