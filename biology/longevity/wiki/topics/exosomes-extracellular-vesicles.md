# Exosomes / extracellular vesicles

Nanoscale membrane-bound vesicles released by cells; carry proteins
and RNAs. Young-stem-cell-derived EVs recapitulate much of the young-
blood rejuvenation effect in a cell-free modality.

## In this wiki
- [[papers/adsc-exosomes-2022]] — young ADSC sEVs improve healthspan
  and lower epigenetic age in old mice.
