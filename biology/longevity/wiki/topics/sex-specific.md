# Sex-specific effects

Many aging interventions have dramatically different effects in male
vs. female mice (and likely humans).

## In this wiki
- [[papers/itp-sex-specific-2025]] — ITP male 5.5% vs. female 1.6%
  lifespan extension; weight loss is a key male-specific mediator.
- [[papers/itp-nia]] — 17α-estradiol is male-only; acarbose is
  male-biased.
