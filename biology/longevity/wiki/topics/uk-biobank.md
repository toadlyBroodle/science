# UK Biobank

~500k deeply phenotyped UK participants. Gated access via UKB
application (approvable for independent researchers), then data download
is straightforward.

## Aging-relevant releases
- **NMR metabolomics (Nightingale)** — ~275k participants
- **Olink proteomics** — ~45k participants (used by
  [[papers/organ-proteomic-clocks-2025]])
- DNA methylation (subset)
- Whole-genome sequencing (~500k)
- EHR + hospital episode statistics for follow-up

## Papers that mined UKB for aging
- [[papers/ukb-nmr-metabolomic-2024]]
- [[papers/organ-proteomic-clocks-2025]]

## Open problems
- Multi-omic integration at UKB scale (proteome × methylome × metabolome)
- Causal inference with genetic instruments (MR scaled across biomarker
  panels)
- Longitudinal rate-of-aging modeling on the ~13k repeat-measurement
  subset
