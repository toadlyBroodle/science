# Hallmarks of aging

Lopez-Otin et al.'s canonical list, updated in 2023 to 12 hallmarks:
genomic instability, telomere attrition, epigenetic alterations, loss
of proteostasis, disabled macroautophagy, deregulated nutrient sensing,
mitochondrial dysfunction, cellular senescence, stem-cell exhaustion,
altered intercellular communication, chronic inflammation, and dysbiosis.
